We are bound by the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information: name, email address, phone number

  • Device information: browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Content you provide: uploaded images, text, ideas, or other materials submitted through our platforms

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you when you register, subscribe, submit forms, or contact us

  • From third-party services you connect, such as Google Calendar or Roblox (username only)
  • From payment processors (Stripe) for transaction records

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our platforms and services
  • Process transactions and send related information including purchase confirmations
  • Send you reminders, notifications, and alerts you have opted into
  • Communicate with you about updates, support, and service-related notices
  • Improve and personalise your experience across our platforms

  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security breaches
